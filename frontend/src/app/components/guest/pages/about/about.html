Transforming Ghana's Energy Landscape

At Tumirails, we are dedicated to building a sustainable and equitable energy future for Ghana, powered by decentralized solar technology and community empowerment.

Our Story & Vision

Tumirails was founded on the belief that access to clean, affordable, and reliable energy is a fundamental right. Witnessing the energy challenges faced by many communities in Ghana, our founders envisioned a decentralized marketplace that would empower individuals and businesses to take control of their energy future.

Our vision is to revolutionize Ghana's energy sector by establishing a nationwide network of digital solar rails. This infrastructure will enable seamless peer-to-peer energy trading, connecting prosumers with excess solar energy to consumers in need. We strive for a future where energy is not just a utility, but a shared resource that fosters economic growth and environmental stewardship.
