# Tumirails Frontend Sitemap

## Overview
This document provides a comprehensive overview of the Tumirails frontend application's structure, including its primary pages, navigation, and core design elements. It serves as a guide for understanding the user flow and overall architecture.


## THEME, COLORS, FONT, ICONS
theme colors: #006994, white,#032230
Primary font: Lato
Secondary: Montserrat

Page: **Solar Fit Check**
 - Navbar
 - Usage Guide Drawer (Open by default): Introductory overview explaining the purpose of the Solar Fit Check tool—helping users determine if their location and consumption are suitable for solar installation. With tutorial tips on how to do a solar fit check
 - Check Workspace panel: Form for users to enter what the fit check will analyze: availability of space for hardware, roof orientation/size/nature, local sunlight hours, current energy usage, electrical appliances, etc.
 - Solar Fit Results Modal: Show results of solar fit: Good fit, Almost There, Not a fit.
 - Solar Requirement recommedation: If not a good fit, Give personalized recommendation of requirements to meet in order to be a good fit, based on solar fit results
 - CTA Section: Prompt to start the Solar Fit Check process or get a personalized quote.
 - Newsletter Section: Option for users to sign up for updates on solar incentives, tips, and Tumi platform news.
 - Footer
