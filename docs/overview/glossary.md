# Glossary

Estimation: Computation of energy needs (kW, kWh, cost) based on site and appliances.
Recommended Hardware: Panels, inverters, batteries suggested for an estimation.
Project: Execution container linking customer, site, installer, hardware, and payments.
Milestone (Post-MVP): A phase of a project with its own payment target.
Split (Post-MVP): Allocation of a payment across provider/installer/platform.
Wallet (Post-MVP): Internal ledgered balance for participants.
kW (kilowatt): Power capacity. kWh (kilowatt-hour): Energy usage over time.
PSP: Payment Service Provider (e.g., Paystack, Flutterwave).
