# Product Vision

Tumi Solar Configurator enables homeowners and businesses to evaluate energy needs, get recommended hardware, and complete solar projects with vetted installers and providers — from estimation to payment — in one streamlined platform.

## Goals

- Make solar sizing simple and trustworthy for non-experts.
- Standardize project execution across customers, installers, and providers.
- Support flexible payments (MVP: upfront; later: milestones, splits, wallets).
- Provide clear visibility of progress, cost, and quality (verification post-MVP).

## Outcomes

- Faster time-to-quote and time-to-install.
- Reduced project disputes (clear milestones/verification later).
- Better selection of hardware fit for each site.
- Reliable financial flows with auditable records.
